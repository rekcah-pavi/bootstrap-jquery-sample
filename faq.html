Frequently Asked Questions

Issue Solution
Payment declined Verify card details and try again. If the issue persists, contact your bank for assistance.
Transaction stuck in processing Contact the customer support of the payment gateway provider and provide transaction details for resolution.
Double charging Contact the customer support of the payment gateway provider and request a refund for the duplicate transaction.
Payment gateway timeout Refresh the page and try again. If the issue persists, try again later or choose an alternative payment method.
Invalid card information Check the card details entered and correct any errors. If the issue persists, try using a different card or contact your bank.
Payment gateway not supported Choose an alternative payment method supported by the book store.
Unexpected error during payment Refresh the page and try again. If the issue persists, contact the customer support of the book store for assistance.
Security concerns Ensure that the website is secure (https://) and the payment gateway is reputable. Avoid sharing sensitive information over unsecured networks.
Payment confirmation email not received Check your spam/junk folder. If not found, contact the customer support of the book store to resend the confirmation email.
Delayed refund Contact the customer support of the book store or payment gateway provider to inquire about the status of the refund.
